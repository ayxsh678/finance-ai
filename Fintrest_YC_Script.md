## [0:00 – 0:15] INTRO — Own who you are

*(Direct eye contact. Calm. Let your age be the hook, not the disclaimer.)*

"Hi, I'm Ayush Verma. I'm a third year B.Tech CSE student —
and I've already built and shipped Fintrest.

Let me show you what it does."

*(Brief pause. Lean in slightly.)*

---

## [0:15 – 0:45] THE PROBLEM — Make them feel it

*(No slides. Just talk. This is where you earn attention.)*

"India has 80 million demat accounts. That number is growing 30% every year.

The average new investor is 26 years old, earning under ₹50,000 a month —
and they are completely on their own.

The tools available to them were built for traders, not beginners.
Bloomberg is for Wall Street. CNBC is for people who already know what a P/E ratio is.

Young Indians investing in Reliance, TCS, and HDFC Bank have no intelligent, simple platform built for them.

That's what I built."

---

## [1:00 – 1:45] THE PRODUCT DEMO — Walk through what you've shipped

*(This is the most important section. Be specific. Name every feature.)*

"Fintrest is a live, AI-powered Indian finance platform — built exclusively for NSE and BSE.

Here's what it does right now:

The **Market** view tracks Indian blue-chips in real time — with live price charts and AI-generated news sentiment scores. Not just what the stock is doing, but *why* — based on what's being written about it today.

The **Fintrest Advisor** is an AI chat built specifically for Indian investing. You can ask it anything — which SIP to start, what Nifty Midcap 150 means, whether HDFC Bank is overvalued — and it answers in plain language, with actual numbers.

The **Watchlist** refreshes live every 60 seconds with sentiment scores alongside price and change — so you're not just watching a number, you're watching the mood around it.

**Compare** lets you put two Indian stocks side by side — Reliance vs TCS, HDFC vs ICICI — and actually understand the difference.

**Portfolio Intelligence** is where it gets serious — you enter your holdings, and the platform generates an AI brief: your risk exposure, where you're overweight, what to watch.

And **Price Alerts** lets you set thresholds — get notified the moment a stock hits your target, above or below.

All of this. India-only. Built in weeks. By one CSE student."

---

## [1:45 – 2:10] THE MARKET — Why this is a massive bet

*(Data mode. Crisp and confident.)*

"India is not a small market for this.

We have the youngest investor demographic in the world.
Zerodha alone onboards over 300,000 new users every month.
The Indian mutual fund industry crossed ₹50 lakh crore in AUM last year.

And yet — there is no AI-native platform built for this audience.
Every major finance tool is either US-first or built for advanced traders.

Fintrest is built from the ground up for the Indian beginner —
in INR, on NSE and BSE, with Indian stocks, Indian context, Indian language."

---

## [2:10 – 2:40] WHY ME — The founder angle

*(Personal now. Slow down. This is where trust is built.)*

"I built this because I was this user.

When I started investing in my second year of college,
I had a Zerodha account and zero idea what to do with it.

I spent hours piecing things together from YouTube videos and Reddit threads —
and I kept thinking: why isn't there a single platform that just *tells* me what's happening with Indian stocks, in plain English, with AI?

I have the technical background to build it.
I have the personal experience to know exactly what it needs to feel like.

And I'm 21 — I am the market."

## [2:40 – 3:00] THE CLOSE — Sharp and confident

*(Slowest part of the video. Eye contact. No rush.)*

"The product is live. The market is real. The timing is now.

India's next 100 million investors are opening their first apps this year —
and they deserve something built for them.

I'm building that with Fintrest.

I'd love to build it faster with YC."
